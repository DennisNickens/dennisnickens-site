Most things don’t fall apart. They’re misunderstood.

Same fight at home. Same friction at work. Same wall in the room you’re trying to lead. The problem isn’t the people across from you. It’s that nobody taught you how to read them. Or yourself.

Leadership

You and your team keep bumping heads.

You think they’re not pulling their weight. They think you don’t listen. The truth is you’re wired to push and they’re wired to consider. Until you understand how they actually receive direction, you’ll keep losing the room and wondering why nobody followed you out.

Teaching

Your students keep failing.

Not because they can’t learn. They learn by doing. You keep handing them books. Match how you teach to how THEY learn, and watch what happens.
