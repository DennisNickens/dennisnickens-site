Doing It God's Way—It Works

Friendship Transitioning Into A Healthy Marriage

“What If Your Marriage Was Built Before You Ever Said ‘I Do’?”

The blueprint for relationships isn’t found in dating apps or Hollywood—it’s been in Genesis all along.

Most people spend more time planning a wedding than preparing for a marriage. They chase chemistry, ignore compatibility, and wonder why the fire fizzles.

Dennis S. Nickens—known as “The Spiritual Romeo”—went straight to the Source. In Doing It God’s Way—It Works, he unpacks the original design for love, partnership, and covenant:

  • →Why God created man FIRST—and what he needed to learn before meeting “her”
  • →The sacred reason Eve was formed from Adam’s rib (and why it changes how you see your spouse)
  • →The difference between a marriage CONTRACT and a blood COVENANT
  • →The 10 questions every couple must answer before the altar—including the ones nobody wants to ask
  • →How to transition from “me” to “we” without losing your God-given purpose

This isn’t another relationship book filled with feel-good fluff. This is a spiritual roadmap for singles ready to stop playing games, couples ready to build on rock instead of sand, and parents ready to show their children what marriage is supposed to look like.

  • Biblical foundation from Genesis to Revelation
  • Practical application for real-world relationships
  • 10 must-ask questions before marriage
  • Guidance on raising relationship-ready children
  • Healing for those recovering from past hurts
  • For singles, couples & ministry leaders
“Stop guessing at love. Start building it God’s way.”

✦ About the Author

Dennis S. Nickens

AKA “The Spiritual Romeo” — Relationship Consultant
