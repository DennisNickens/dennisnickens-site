You Call Yourself a Friend

How well do you really know the people in your life — and do they really know you? You Call Yourself a Friend is a card game that goes beyond small talk and surface friendships. Perfect for friend groups, small groups, church gatherings, or anyone looking to deepen their relationships beyond the scroll.
