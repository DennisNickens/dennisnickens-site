More Than a Coach — A Guide Who's Been There

My name is Dennis Nickens, and I've spent over a decade walking alongside people through some of the most vulnerable, challenging, and ultimately rewarding journeys of their lives — their relationships.

I understand what it feels like to be stuck in patterns that hurt you, to love someone deeply but not know how to show it, and to wonder if real, lasting connection is actually possible. I've been there. And I've come out the other side with something powerful — clarity, tools, and a calling.

I became Spiritual Romeo not because of a title, but because of a transformation. The "Spiritual" reflects the deeper work — understanding ourselves, our wounds, and our worth. The "Romeo" represents the pursuit — the courage to love fully, boldly, and intentionally.

Today, I bring that same combination to every client I work with: spiritual depth meets practical strategy. Whether you're healing from heartbreak, struggling in your marriage, or simply ready to build a love life you're proud of — I'm here for that journey with you.
