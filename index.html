The Concept That Changes Everything

You Both Have a Love Tank.
Are You Filling It With the Right Fuel?

Every person has an emotional "love tank." When it's full, life thrives. When it runs empty — disconnection, resentment, and arguments follow. The problem isn't a lack of effort. It's the wrong fuel.

💬
Words of Affirmation
Heartfelt compliments and encouragement. "I'm proud of you" fills the tank instantly.
⚡
Acts of Service
Cooking a meal, handling a task without being asked — love in motion, not words.
🎁
Receiving Gifts
Not materialism — it's the symbol. "I was thinking about you" expressed through a gesture.
⏰
Quality Time
Full, undivided attention. Phone down, eyes forward — presence, not just proximity.
🤍
Physical Touch
A hug that lingers, a hand on the shoulder — touch communicates safety and love.
"Most couples aren't falling out of love — they're filling each other's tanks with the wrong fuel. You pour in Acts of Service. They're starving for Words of Affirmation. Both of you are trying. Neither of you feels loved. That gap is exactly what we fix."
— Dennis Nickens, Spiritual Romeo
