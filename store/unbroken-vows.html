Unbroken Vows Keeping It Together

By Dennis S. Nickens, AKA "The Spiritual Romeo"

Marriage isn't just a ceremony — it's a covenant. But when life gets hard, when communication breaks down, when the distance between two people feels impossible to cross, the vows you made can start to feel like a distant memory. Unbroken Vows is for the couple who still believes their marriage is worth fighting for.

Dennis S. Nickens walks you through the real, honest work of keeping your marriage together — not just surviving, but thriving. Learn how to renew your commitment when love feels like a choice, rebuild trust after it has been broken, and rediscover the connection that first brought you together. This book gives you practical, faith-grounded tools to strengthen your bond and honor the vows you made before God.

Your vows were meant to be unbreakable. Let's keep them that way.
